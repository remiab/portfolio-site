Lodestone

a Queer Bookshop and Social Space

The Lodestone will be a bookshop and cafe for the local LGBTQ+ community. Connect with others over a book and a coffee, or make friends with boardgames and tea. Just fancy some time to yourself? Curl up with a book in one of our reading nooks.

We're also planning evening art classes, games nights and film screenings.
